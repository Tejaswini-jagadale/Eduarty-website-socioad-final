EduArty-Academy of Excellence

The journey of EduArty started in 2009 with an intention of making learning easy and fun for students. Our focus has always been to teach differently. We observed that math was kept on a very high pedestal by schools, parents etc that it was difficult for kids to meet the expectations as far as marks are concerned. This was when we introduced Vedic Mathematics. However EduArty has always been focusing beyond school and so we also added career oriented and Environmental understanding Coaching, Where we introduced Newspaper weaving and Recycling techniques.

Key Features

  • EduArty gives a platform for all students to choose different subjects.
  • Overall growth of a student is our priority. To make studies fun is what we are working towards.

Values

We work on 5 Core Values, Truthfulness, Fairness, Dignity, Responsibility and Freedom. Our teachings are founded on ethics, whether it is teacher- student relationship or the relationship of a teacher with our Institute. We believe, Dignity is Respect.

Vision

  • Our vision is to make sure that each child develops a curiosity of learning, discover their interests and grow the love for learning.
  • We also desire to have strong families through parent support/fellowship and skills training.

Mission

  • To provide a learning environment which help our students to achieve academic excellence in professional education at par with leading institutions.
  • Focus on Practical aspects of the subjects and integrate the same in theoretical training.
  • Actively encourage students to retain logical subjects
  • Foster love for learning.
  • Encourage our teachers to to try new and exciting things to build a solid foundation for the subject.
